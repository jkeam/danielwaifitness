Peter

"I've only been training with Dan since February 2014, but I can honestly say its been a life changing experience! I always had a pretty self-disciplined training program over the years (and there's been a few of those as I'm one of Dan's older clients - something he loves to remind me of), mainly focused on running, with a gym session every week or two. To that end, I've tried a number of trainers in the past but frankly got bored pretty quickly. Prior to meeting Dan, my experience of other trainers is that they expect you to fit in to their training regime with their goals - that can be interesting for bit but soon becomes monotonous. Not so with Dan, who I now look forward to training with twice a week.

Dan is a fan of training and his enthusiasm is infectious. Moreover his knowledge and approach is holistic, combining fitness and strength conditioning with nutritional advice if you want it! Most importantly though, Dan sees his profession as a 'people business', and treats his clients accordingly. That means his clients' goals and motivations come first, and the program he designs for them is bespoke to each. He also prides and challenges himself on no two sessions being the same - something which continues to amaze me. When I started training with Dan, my goals were to get stronger, not bulk up, and get a 6 pack by the time I'm 50. Looks like we are achieving this about a year early, but I intend to keep working with him because he's the best in the business I've come across. He's a tough task master no doubt, but above all else its interesting and fun."
